Multiple Banneker County missing persons cases thought to be linked

Published: Feb. 01, 2016, 11:30 a.m.


By David Garcia | davidgar@upny.com

The New York State Police Department is actively investigating a series of unresolved missing person cases in Banneker County, New York. The incidents have occurred on a recurring cycle, with individuals vanishing at an approximate 17-year interval, dating back to the 1870s. Despite extensive efforts, law enforcement authorities have been unable to solve the enigma surrounding these disappearances. The following is an official record of the names and disappearance years of the individuals involved. The New York State Police Department urges anyone with information related to these cases to come forward and provide any relevant details that could lead to the resolution of these perplexing cases.
